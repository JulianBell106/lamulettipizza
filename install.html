Android — Add to Home Screen
  1. 1 Scan the QR code — Chrome opens the ordering page.
  2. 2 Tap "Add to Home screen" in the banner at the bottom of Chrome.
  3. 3 Tap Install — the app icon appears on your home screen.
  4. 4 Open it any time to order ahead — no App Store, no account required.
On iPhone? Open the link in Safari, tap the Share button, then "Add to Home Screen". Ask staff if you need a hand.
